360°Artist Development
"Talent is the spark. We build the stage, the sound, and the story around it."

Triple 4 Entertainment was founded on a simple belief: great artists aren't discovered by accident, they're developed on purpose. We scout raw talent in clubs, on stages, and in voice memos sent at 2am, then put real structure behind it — vocal coaching, choreography, studio time, and a release plan that actually gets the music heard.

We're not a singles factory. Every artist on our roster gets a development plan built around their voice, their genre, and their goals — whether that's a debut single or a full album rollout.

The Artist Pipeline

From First Audition To Finished Release

Four stages, one roof. We carry artists through every part of the process so they can focus on the music.

I

Discover

Our A&R team scouts new talent through open submissions, live showcases, and referrals across New York, Atlanta, and Miami. We listen for voice, presence, and potential — not just polish.

Open SubmissionsLive ShowcasesA&R Scouting

Develop

Vocal TrainingChoreographyPerformance Coaching

Record
